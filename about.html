Our Story

Founded in 2024, Paolo's Paws emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: pets deserve the highest quality care, delivered with personal attention and professional expertise.

Our founder, Paolo, has over 15 years of experience in professional pet care. What started as a passion for animals grew into a mission to provide the community with reliable, loving pet care services.
